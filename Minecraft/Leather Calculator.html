This tool allows you to generate a crafting recipe for any craftable color of leather armor in Minecraft Java


or

The rgb-value of the 16 basic colors available changed two times since the introduction of dyeable leather armor

There is a total of 5,713,438 craftable colors in versions 1.12+

There is a total of 4,200,779 craftable colors in versions 1.4.3 - 1.11.2

There is a total of 4,271,547 craftable colors in version 1.4.2

The rgb colour space contains 256^3 = 16 777 216 total colors
